# Outdoor Lighting Design using DIALux

## Project Overview

This project presents the design and simulation of an outdoor lighting system for a modern urban environment using DIALux Evo. The layout includes a main roadway, pedestrian pathways, landscaped areas, and surrounding buildings.

The objective of this project is to ensure **safe visibility, uniform illumination, and energy-efficient lighting performance** while complying with standard lighting design practices.

---

## Project Objectives

* Ensure safe vehicular and pedestrian movement
* Achieve uniform light distribution across all areas
* Minimize glare and dark spots
* Optimize energy efficiency
* Enhance visual comfort and aesthetics

---

## Site Description

The designed area consists of:

* Main road for vehicular movement
* Pedestrian walkways and crossings
* High-rise buildings surrounding the site
* Landscaped green areas with trees

The lighting layout is developed to provide complete coverage of all critical zones within the site.

---

## Tools & Software Used

* DIALux Evo (Lighting Design & Simulation)
* AutoCAD (for layout preparation – if applicable)

---

## Design Approach

* Strategic placement of luminaires along the roadway and pathways
* Optimization of pole height and spacing for uniform coverage
* Consideration of glare control for driver and pedestrian comfort
* Focus on energy-efficient lighting solutions
* Proper alignment of luminaires to avoid light pollution

---

## Standards Considered

* EN 13201 – Road Lighting Standards
* General outdoor lighting design guidelines

---

## Key Results

* Achieved uniform illumination across the main road
* Adequate lighting levels for pedestrian safety
* Reduced dark spots and improved visibility
* Energy-efficient luminaire arrangement
